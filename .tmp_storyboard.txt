Knowledge Check
Let’s pause for a moment.
A customer says:
“I want something special… I just don’t
want it to feel over the top.”
What would you say next?
A) “Then you should look at our rarest
options — that’s what makes it special.”
B) “Tell me a bit about the moment — and
how you’d like to wear the piece — then I’ll
show you a few options that fit.”
C) “Let’s start with the newest pieces —
those are usually the best choice.”
D) “If it must be special, the higher the price,
the better.”
(Pause)
The best answer is B.
At 1896, “special” isn’t only about price or
rarity — it’s about meaning, and how the
piece fits the customer’s life.

Knowledge Check
A customer is browsing quietly and says,
“I’m just looking.”
What’s the best next step?
• A) Start explaining the full range
• B) Give them space and check back
gently
• C) Ask about their budget
immediately
(Pause)
The best answer is B.
A 1896 experience is customer-led.
We respect the customer’s pace and allow
the conversation to unfold naturally.
